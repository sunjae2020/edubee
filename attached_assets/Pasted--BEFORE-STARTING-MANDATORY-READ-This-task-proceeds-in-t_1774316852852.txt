## System Architecture Context (Must Understand Before Proceeding)

### Core Accounting Engine (already implemented)

The accounting system operates as follows:
payment_headers (PAY-XXXXXXXX)
└── payment_lines (split by line_type)
└── journal_entries (auto-generated double-entry)
└── chart_of_accounts (CoA master)

**Journal Entry Auto-Generation Rules by line_type:**

| line_type | DR (Debit) | CR (Credit) | Description |
|-----------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| trust_receipt | 1200 Trust Account | 2100 AP — Tuition Payable | Student payment into trust |
| service_fee | 1100 Cash & Bank | 3400/3500/3900 Service Fee | Direct revenue recognition |
| trust_disbursement | 2100 AP — Tuition | 1200 Trust Account | School remittance from trust |
| commission_transfer | 1200 Trust Account | 3100/3200 Commission | Pre-deduct commission split |
| commission_receipt | 1100 Cash & Bank | 1300 AR — Commission | Full remit commission receipt |
| sub_agent | 4100 Sub-agent Comm | 1100 Cash & Bank | Sub-agent payout |
| super_agent | 4200 Super-agent Comm | 1100 Cash & Bank | Super-agent payout |
| referral_fee | 4300 Referral Fee | 1100 Cash & Bank | Referral partner payout |
| incentive | 5300 Incentive | 1100 Cash & Bank | Staff incentive payout |
| salary | 5200 Salary | 1100 Cash & Bank | Staff salary |

DR total must always equal CR total — server blocks save if mismatched.
All operations must be executed as a single atomic DB transaction.

**Camp-specific CoA codes:**
| Code | Name | Used For |
|------|------|---------|
| 3500 | Service Fee — Camp | Camp AR revenue |
| 4600 | Direct — Tour Cost | camp_tour_mgt AP |
| 4700 | Direct — Other | camp_institute_mgt AP |

### contract_products = Payment Schedule

`contract_products` is the single source of truth for the payment
schedule. No separate payment schedule table exists or is needed.
contract_products (1 row = 1 payment instalment)
├── AR Side: ar_amount, ar_due_date, ar_status, coa_ar_code
│            (scheduled → invoiced → partial → paid → overdue)
└── AP Side: ap_amount, ap_due_date, ap_status, coa_ap_code
(pending → ready → paid → overdue)

The AR/AP Tracker page manages this via three tabs:
- Tab 1: AR Tracker (ar_status-based)
- Tab 2: AP Tracker (ap_status-based)
- Tab 3: Payment Schedule (contract_products read-only view)

### Multi-Cost Commission Structure

For each contract_products line, multiple cost obligations can exist
simultaneously via product_cost_lines:
contract_products (1 line = 1 revenue line)
└── product_cost_lines (1..N cost obligations per line)
├── sub_agent commission  (cost_type: sub_agent)
├── super_agent commission (cost_type: super_agent)
├── referral fee          (cost_type: referral)
├── staff incentive       (cost_type: incentive)
└── discount              (cost_type: discount)

Sub-agent: partner using main agency's school contract
  → main agency takes 10–90% of total revenue
Super-agent: main agency using another agency's school contract
  → license/referral fee = 10–90% of revenue
All cost obligations link to chart_of_accounts via coa_code field.

### Account Types (Extended)

| DB Value | Role | Ledger Connection |
|----------|------|------------------|
| student | Student (service recipient) | AR (tuition collection) |
| school | Partner school | AP (remittance) + AR (commission) |
| sub_agency | Sub-agent partner | AP (sub commission) |
| super_agency | Super-agent partner | AP (super commission) |
| supplier | Homestay/pickup/insurance provider | AP (service cost) |
| staff | Internal staff | AP (salary + incentive) |
| branch | Branch office | Independent branch accounting |
| organisation | Other organisations | Context-dependent |

### Services Module — Others Category

The Services page has been extended with an "Others" category
beyond the original 6 modules (study_abroad, pickup, accommodation,
internship, settlement, guardian).

Others service type is determined by Product Group.
Tab structure for Others service detail:
- Overview (service type via Product Group, title, period,
  assigned staff, partner account, status)
- Documents (linked to files table)
- Bill (billing and cost info, partner cost AP, Invoice linkage)
- Notes (memo and comment history)

All services share the same pattern:
- No separate billing per service
- All AR/AP managed through Contract
- Contract detail page → Services tab → individual service panels
